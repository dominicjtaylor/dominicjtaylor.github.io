Galaxy Formation in Cosmological Simulations

Formation efficiency is principally affected by stellar and AGN feedback. Here I explored the second order influences.

Stellar Populations in the Bulge of the Milky Way

The Milky Way has accreted stars from many systems throughout its life. Is Terzan 5 the remnant of a building block of the Galactic bulge?

Star Formation at high-z

Accurate measures of molecular hydrogen gas masses require knowledge of the excitation ladder of CO. Here I am testing the parameterisation of the relation.

Changing-Look AGN

AGN exhibit a variety of variations in their X-ray and Optical properties, on top of those caused by geometry. Here, I searched for new CLAGN using decades-worth of stacked images.
