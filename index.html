Galaxy Formation in Cosmological Simulations

Formation efficiency is principally affected by stellar and AGN feedback. Here I explored the second order influences using EAGLE.

Stellar Populations in the Bulge of the Milky Way

The Milky Way has accreted stars from many systems. Is Terzan 5 the remnant of a building block of the Galactic bulge? Evidence from SDSS APOGEE.

Star Formation at high-z

Accurate measures of molecular hydrogen gas masses require knowledge of the excitation ladder of CO. Here I am testing the physical drivers with ALMA.

Changing-Look AGN

AGN exhibit variations in their X-ray and Optical properties on top of those caused by geometry. Here, I searched for new CLAGN using decades-worth of stacked images from XMM-Newton.
